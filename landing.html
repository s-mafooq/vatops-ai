What VatOps does
Everything a UK finance team needs.
Nothing it doesn't.
AI extraction
OCR + Mistral LLM extracts supplier, date, totals, VAT, and line items from any invoice format. Field-level confidence scoring flags uncertainty before review.
↗ PaddleOCR · Mistral 7B
UK VAT classification
Applies HMRC rules automatically. Distinguishes missing VAT from zero-rated. Flags anomalies and mismatched totals before they become compliance issues.
↗ HMRC MTD 2026
Bank reconciliation
Upload bank CSV or Excel exports. Fuzzy-matches invoices to transactions with explainable scoring shows exactly why each match was chosen.
↗ CSV · XLSX · XLS
